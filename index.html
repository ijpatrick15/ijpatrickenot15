About me

Quick introduction

Meet IJ, a talented individual who graduated with an Information Technology degree from Bukidnon State University in 2022. IJ was bestowed with the prestigious "Student Service Award" in acknowledgment of his outstanding contributions and achievements.

Notably, during his High School years at Malaybalay City National Highschool , he was awarded the prestigious "Artist of the Year" title in 2015, showcasing his exceptional creative abilities.

With a passion for creating diverse kinds and formats of videos, IJ actively participates in various video competitions. His dedication and skills earned him the distinction of being a "National Champion" in the Travel and Experience Video Competition at the STI Tagisan ng Talino Nationals in 2018.

IJ has a remarkable background as a Former Photographer, Videographer, and Video Editor at Bukidnon State University (Malaybalay City). He garnered tremendous success at the ASEAN STARS GALA 2023 Competitions , with "3 of his video submissions being crowned Champions".

Now, he embarks on an exciting new journey as a freelancer, eagerly exploring opportunities to create innovative and captivating video projects. Let's connect and collaborate with IJ to bring your video ideas to life!

My Services

I believe in Experiment and learn | Rather then learn and learn.

Digital TV Format

I edit raw video footage, adding effects and graphics to create engaging content. I work with the production team to meet technical requirements for TV and digital platforms. Goal: visually appealing content that connects with viewers.

Music Video

As a music video creator and editor, my role is to bring the artist's vision to life through visually stunning videos. I develop concepts, direct shoots, and edit footage with specialized software. My goal is to engage viewers, enhance the musical experience, and promote the artist's work while aligning with their creative vision and brand.

Advertisements

As an ad video editor, I create compelling and visually stunning ads using raw footage, graphics, and music. My goal is to resonate with the target audience and drive action, collaborating closely with the advertising agency and marketing team to deliver an effective message.

Animated Intros

As an animated graphics/video editor, I create engaging content by combining animation, footage, and effects. I aim to captivate viewers while meeting technical requirements and client expectations.

Formal Video Presentation

As a video presentation editor for corporate clients, my main responsibility is to transform raw footage into a polished, professional video that meets the client's needs. This includes reviewing footage, selecting the best shots, and creating a coherent narrative. Additionally, I add graphics, animations, and text to enhance the video's impact and highlight important points.

Interview / Podcast Video

As a specialized editor for interview and podcast videos, my main responsibility is to turn raw footage into a captivating final product. I carefully select the best shots and sound bites while ensuring visual appeal. I also handle audio adjustments, adding music, sound effects, graphics, and text to enhance the video's quality and emphasize key points.

Gaming Videos

As a gaming video editor, my expertise lies in crafting captivating and visually stunning gaming content. I possess a keen eye for detail and excel at piecing together thrilling gameplay sequences, adding dynamic transitions, special effects, and compelling soundtracks to create immersive and engaging gaming experiences for viewers. My goal is to enhance the overall gaming narrative, delivering top-notch videos that entertain and delight gaming enthusiasts.
